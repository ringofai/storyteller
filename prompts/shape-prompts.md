# SHAPE Prompts — AI Prompt Library

## For Structuring a Story

```
Here is my story hook: [HOOK]

Help me structure it using the Three-Act Structure.

Act 1 (What Is): Suggest how to establish the status quo.
- Who is the hero?
- What's at stake?
- What's the world like before the story starts?

Act 2 (The Gap): Suggest the inciting incident.
- What creates tension between "what is" and "what could be"?
- What obstacle appears?
- What's the moment of greatest doubt?

Act 3 (New Bliss): Suggest the resolution.
- How does the hero change?
- What's the call to action?
```

## For Finding the Character Spine

```
Here is my story hero: [HERO DESCRIPTION]

Help me identify their spine — the dominant, often unconscious goal that drives their actions.

1. What does this hero want more than ANYTHING?
2. What's standing in their way?
3. If they got what they wanted, would the story end? (If no, you haven't found the spine yet.)
4. If they lost what they wanted, would the story end? (If yes, you found it.)

Write the spine as one sentence: "[HERO]'s spine is [GOAL]."
```

## For the Golden Circle (Why Framework)

```
Here is my story topic: [TOPIC]

Help me apply Simon Sinek's Golden Circle:

WHAT — What does this story explicitly describe?
HOW — How is this different from other stories on the same topic?
WHY — Why does this story EXIST? What belief or purpose drives it?

Communicate from the inside out: start with WHY, then HOW, then WHAT.
```

## For Bilingual Structure Adaptation

```
Here is my story outline in [LANGUAGE]: [OUTLINE]

This story works in [SOURCE LANGUAGE]. Help me adapt it for [TARGET LANGUAGE].

Consider:
1. Does the three-act structure feel natural in [TARGET LANGUAGE]?
2. Would a more circular or implicit structure work better?
3. Is the "Why" framed for an individualist or collectivist audience?
4. How does the emotional arc need to shift?
```
