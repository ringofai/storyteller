# Workshop Blueprint: 2-Hour Storytelling Session

A compressed introduction to the Storyteller Framework. Suitable for secondary or university students.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, participants will:
- Understand the 5-stage Storyteller Framework
- Write a story hook and three-act outline
- Practise vocal delivery techniques
- Give and receive structured feedback

## Materials

- Printed handouts of the framework
- Timer
- Optional: LEGO bricks for warm-up exercises
- Access to AI tool (optional)

## Agenda

| Time | Duration | Activity | Stage | Facilitator Notes |
|:-----|:---------|:---------|:------|:------------------|
| 0:00-0:05 | 5 min | **Opening:** "Why Storytelling" — the 225/500 wpm fact, the 200,000-year history of stories | — | Start with a small story about storytelling to model the craft |
| 0:05-0:10 | 5 min | **Warm-up:** "The Three-Brick Story" — each person builds a 3-brick model representing a story they want to tell | SHAPE | LSP-inspired. Let them share without pressure. |
| 0:10-0:20 | 10 min | **Mini-lecture:** The 5 Stages — HOOK → SHAPE → FLESH → JUDGE → LAND | ALL | Visual diagram on screen. Emphasise: "Start from the ending." |
| 0:20-0:35 | 15 min | **Exercise 1: HOOK** — Write your ending first. Find the gap. Share with a partner. | HOOK | Use the hook exercise from Stage 1. Partner shares feedback. |
| 0:35-0:50 | 15 min | **Exercise 2: SHAPE** — Three-act outline. Character spine. | SHAPE | Use the "Once upon a time..." template from Stage 2 |
| 0:50-1:05 | 15 min | **Exercise 3: FLESH** — Expand one act into a paragraph. Add one sensory detail. | FLESH | Prompt: "What did it look, sound, feel, smell, taste like?" |
| 1:05-1:15 | 10 min | **Midpoint share:** Read your opening paragraph to a partner | ALL | |
| 1:15-1:25 | 10 min | **Exercise 4: JUDGE** — Peer feedback using Three Loves / One Suggestion | JUDGE | Use the peer feedback form from the rubric |
| 1:25-1:40 | 15 min | **Exercise 5: LAND** — Vocal warm-up. Practise first 60 seconds. | LAND | Do the 6 HAIL warm-ups together (90 sec). Then pair practise. |
| 1:40-1:50 | 10 min | **Quick showcase:** 3 volunteers share their 60-second story | LAND | |
| 1:50-2:00 | 10 min | **Closing circle:** "One sentence I want remembered" — each person shares their story's single idea | JUDGE | Powerful closing ritual. Take a photo of the circle. |
