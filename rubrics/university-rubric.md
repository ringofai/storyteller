# Storytelling Rubric — University Students (Ages 18-22)

Use this rubric to assess student stories across all 5 stages of the Storyteller Framework.

| Criterion | Developing (1) | Competent (2) | Strong (3) | Excellent (4) |
|:----------|:---------------|:--------------|:-----------|:--------------|
| **Hook** | Gets attention | Creates a clear tension gap between "what is" and "what could be" | Hook type is intentional and matched to the audience | Hook is surprising, memorable, and sets up the entire emotional arc |
| **Structure** | Has a visible arc | Three-act structure is clear with balanced sections | Transitions between acts are smooth and purposeful | Structure is invisible to the audience — they feel the journey, not the framework |
| **Fleshing Out** | Script or output is complete | Uses at least one metaphor or sensory detail | Multi-modal approach (e.g. script + storyboard + visual assets) | Production choices serve the story's emotional goal — nothing is decorative |
| **Vulnerability** | A personal element is included | Vulnerability feels earned, not performative | Vulnerability is specific, contextual, and serves the story's purpose | Vulnerability is the engine of audience connection |
| **Landing** | Ends with a point | Ends with a clear call to action | Delivery is rehearsed and feels natural | The audience remembers one sentence; everything else supports it |
| **AI Use** | AI was used somewhere in the process | AI use is documented and transparent | AI amplified the human voice — the story wouldn't exist without the human | Story is unmistakably human but would be weaker without AI's amplification |
| **Reflection** | Describes what happened | Describes what was learned | Connects learning to storytelling craft principles | Identifies specific growth areas for the next story |

## Scoring Guide

| Total Points | Level |
|:-------------|:------|
| 7-12 | Developing — foundations are in place. Focus on one area per story. |
| 13-18 | Competent — solid storyteller. Push toward specificity and intentionality. |
| 19-24 | Strong — advanced craft. Experiment with riskier vulnerability or cross-format production. |
| 25-28 | Excellent — publishable, presentable, teachable. |

## Self-Assessment Questions

After submitting, answer:

1. **The Single-Idea Test:** In one sentence, what is my story about?
2. **The Worth-Sharing Test:** Who benefits from this story besides me?
3. **The AI Check:** Where did AI help? Where did I override AI to keep my voice?
4. **The Growth Question:** What would I do differently in my next story?
